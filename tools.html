Simulation

Models Stop Work Authority (SWA) decisions. Applies Prospect Theory and Hyperbolic Discounting to calculate risk-avoidance behaviors and simulate crew response rates under varying operational pressures.

04

Safety Identity Analysis

NLP Model

Analyzes industry practitioner sentiment. Clusters unstructured survey responses using SBERT embeddings, K-Means, and UMAP to map semantic archetypes and compare ISO 45001 compliance alignment against Safety-II resilience concepts.

View Analysis →
05

Regulens for OSHA

Browser Extension

Chrome extension that modifies the osha.gov DOM. Detects regulations, embeds official Letters of Interpretation directly adjacent to target clauses, and resolves regulatory cross-references inline.

OSHA Incident Classification Model

Streamlit · ML

Classifies unstructured injury narratives. Compares Logistic Regression, XGBoost, and BERT models trained on OSHA Severe Injury Reports (2015–2025) to map descriptions to the OIICS v2.01 standard.

View Application →
07

Dropped Object Assessment

iOS App

Native iOS app calculating dropped-object risk (DROPS). Computes impact energy (Joules) from mass and drop height, maps outputs to risk levels, and generates on-site PDF assessment reports.
